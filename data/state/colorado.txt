CDPHE Immunization Educational Materials Now Available
Senate Bill 25-194: Sunset Dental Practice Act
requires that Dentists and Dental Hygienists, who administer immunizations, distribute immunization educational materials and recommendations developed by the Department of Public Health and Environment (CDPHE) to their patients. CDPHE has provided the
Dental Patient Vaccine Handout
, which meets the requirements of CRS 12-220-305(1)(t)(II)(E) and CRS 12-220-504(1)(d)(II)(E).
